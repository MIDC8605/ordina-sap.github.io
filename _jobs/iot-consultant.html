What?

As a SAP IoT consultant, you will be collaborating with startups, corporates and government agencies in order to develop projects and platforms to help create new IoT data marketplaces and innovation testbeds, lowering existing barriers for sharing of IoT data among organizations. Together with your engagement team you develop complex analytical solutions that meet the needs of the client in new, innovative ways. You quickly adopt emerging technological trends (such as predictive analytics, business analytics, or machine learning) to develop creative solutions. You support business requirements and learn to identify them, you conduct value discovery tracks, develop functional and technical design documents, prototypes and test solutions, train users, and define support procedures. You develop ‘thought ware’ and ‘point-of-view’ documents related to the Internet of Things.

What are we looking for?

  • You are an IoT technologist with a deep passion for this fast growing area
  • You have a master degree, preferably in computer engineering, and you have a first professional experience
  • Knowledge or experience in emerging IoT systems and corresponding IoT middleware platforms is a plus
  • You have solid understanding of IoT architecture (from connectivity to aggregation layers)
  • You have a first programming experience in web scripting/web services and Java
  • Cloud knowledge (SAP Cloud Platform or other cloud environments) is a plus
  • You have a good understanding of data analytics

What we offer

  • You’re part of a company that is a SAP Gold Partner and a SAP Certified Center of Expertise
  • You will contribute to challenging projects at top international and Belgian companies from diverse sectors
  • You will work in a high-tech environment with a focus on knowledge and innovation, an open no-nonsense business culture with space for personal initiative
  • You keep your technical and social skills up-to-date at our Ordina Academy and are supported in obtaining certificates
  • You will receive a competitive salary package with additional benefits such as a company car, laptop, insurance package, luncheon vouchers, expense account, etc.
  • You can take part in fun business and team building activities
