What?

As SAP UI5 and Fiori developer you are part of the technical team within the SAP unit. You work for customers who need your technical expertise on the subject and the development of applications that fulfil their requirements and are also user-friendly. In addition, you will always take the customer’s existing IT environment into account.

What are we looking for?

  • You have practical experience with SAP UI5, Fiori and developing applications in a business context
  • You are willing to develop your knowledge on this topic further and obtain additional certificates if required
  • You communicate fluently and clearly both with colleagues and customers
  • You are convincingly able to formulate your own opinion and solutions
  • You can work well in a team, in which you share your knowledge and also learn more but can also work independently

What we offer

  • You will contribute to challenging projects at top international and Belgian companies from diverse sectors
  • You will work in a high-tech environment with a focus on knowledge and innovation, an open no-nonsense business culture with space for personal initiative
  • You keep your technical and social skills up-to-date at our Ordina Academy and are supported in obtaining certificates
  • You will receive a competitive salary package with additional benefits such as a company car, laptop, insurance package, luncheon vouchers, expense account, etc.
  • You can take part in fun business and team building activities
