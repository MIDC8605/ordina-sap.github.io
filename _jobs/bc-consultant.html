What?

As a SAP BC consultant you are in charge of all SAP application management for various clients. You ensure the continuous availability of the SAP systems and you ensure continuity of the system during installations, upgrades and other SAP-related activities. You are happy to think along with your customers about the deployment and implementation of new SAP technologies.

What are we looking for?

  • You have knowledge of database management systems (Oracle, SQL Server, DB2, …)
  • You have knowledge of operating systems (Windows Server, Linux, Unix, …)
  • You have knowledge of SAP (Netweaver, S/4HANA, Solution Manager, …)
  • You are analytical and think in a problem-solving way
  • You are interested in the latest technological developments in the field of SAP

What we offer

  • You will contribute to challenging projects at top international and Belgian companies from diverse sectors
  • You will work in a high-tech environment with a focus on knowledge and innovation, an open no-nonsense business culture with space for personal initiative
  • You keep your technical and social skills up-to-date at our Ordina Academy and are supported in obtaining certificates
  • You will receive a competitive salary package with additional benefits such as a company car, laptop, insurance package, luncheon vouchers, expense account, etc.
  • You can take part in fun business and team building activities
