About SAP@Ordina

Want to develop a new SAP solution? Or modify your existing SAP applications? Our consultants and teams build innovative solutions using HANA, ABAP coding and SAP Leonardo. To support them in that mission, we have several Competence Centers. From within those Competence Centers, we provide coaching to the employee and expert advice towards our customer. In order to keep in sync with the latest technologies and the latest trends, we frequently visit conferences around the globe.
